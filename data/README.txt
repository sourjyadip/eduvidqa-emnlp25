The following columns are present in the csv file:


- url: The video URL associated with the question.
- id: The video ID extracted from the URL.
- question: The question asked by the learner, extracted from Youtube comments.
- answer: The answer to the question, verified to ensure correctness.


We suggest using yt-dlp (https://github.com/yt-dlp/yt-dlp) for downloading the videos.
